Melbourne Loves ... meetups!

@MuMuMelb   #MuMu15

T'was a few nights before mixer..

We're thrilled to announce Melbourne's inaugural, combined end of year mixer: 'MeetupMixup'! It's a fantastic opportunity to relax with a cold beverage and celebrate a year of meetups!

The event is open to all, and we'll be gathering on December 15th for an evening of celebrations to round out the year and welcome the holiday season. Get in quick, as spaces are limited!

We're glad to be hosting, amongst others, members of phpMelb, Open Knowledge Melbourne, Melbourne Silicon Beach, Meteor Melbourne, Digital Poets Society - Symfony2 Meetup, Melbourne Symfony2 Developers, and Linux Users of Victoria user groups. If your user group wants to officially join in, send us a tweet and we'll add you to this list!

Code of Conduct

MeetupMixup is dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone. We do not tolerate harassment of meetup participants in any form. Meetup participants violating these rules may be sanctioned or expelled from the meetup without a refund at the discretion of the conference organizers.

A contact number will be publicised at the venue, should anyone need assistance.
